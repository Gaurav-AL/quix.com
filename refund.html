Refund & Cancellation Policy

📅 Last updated: March 12, 2026 · 👤 Developer: Gaurav Gaurav · 📦 com.quix.app
💬

For billing issues or refund requests, email support@quix.app within 7 days of the charge. We respond within 24–48 business hours.

1 Subscriptions

Quix offers monthly Pro subscriptions purchased through Google Play.

1 Month₹99 / month
3 Months₹249 (₹83/mo — save ₹48)
6 Months₹449 (₹75/mo — save ₹145)
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled before the renewal date
  • Cancel from Google Play → Subscriptions → Quix → Cancel
  • Access continues until end of the current paid period after cancellation
  • Cancellation alone does not trigger a refund for the current period

2 Pay Per Generation

Pay-per-generation purchases (₹19 per AI quiz) are non-refundable once the quiz has been successfully delivered. If a generation fails due to a technical error on our end, a full refund or credit will be issued.

3 Eligible for a Refund

  • App was inaccessible for more than 48 hours due to a server outage on our end
  • A duplicate charge occurred for the same period
  • A pay-per-generation purchase failed to produce any output
  • You were charged after cancelling your subscription

4 Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Forgetting to cancel before auto-renewal
  • Partial use of a subscription period
  • Device incompatibility issues

5 How to Request a Refund

Email support@quix.app with your Google Play account, charge date and amount, and reason. We respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds process in 5–7 business days.

6 Google Play Refunds

You may also request a refund directly from Google within 48 hours of purchase at Google Play Order History.
